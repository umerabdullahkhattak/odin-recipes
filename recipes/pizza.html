Pizza

Description

typically of flattened bread dough spread with a savory mixture usually including tomatoes and cheese and often other toppings and baked.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Pizza dough
  • Floor
  • Tomato
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough
  2. Prepare the pizza base
  3. Chop all the vegetables for the pizza
  4. Spread the sauce and veggies on the base
  5. Bake the pizza at 250 degree Celsius for 10 minutes
